Registration Note

The registration to the HCSE conference 2014 is possible via the online registration form or on-site. For applying for the early registration fee, registration must be completed until August 18, 2014. Otherwise, the late registration fee applies. The fees for on-site registration need to be paid in cash or by credit card (VISA or MASTERCARD). Checks cannot be accepted.

Payment Policy

If you have selected the SEPA bank transfer, payment must be received within two weeks after the registration. Otherwise your registration will be canceled! If the conference fees have not been paid until the beginning of the conference, they need to be paid on-site in cash or by credit card (VISA or MASTERCARD). Checks cannot be accepted.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations must be submitted in writing. All cancellations received in writing on or before August 08, 2014 are entitled to a refund, minus a 50.00€ processing fee. No refunds will be given to registrants who cancel their registration after August 08, 2014 or who fail to attend the event. Substitutions for paid registrants may be made at any time without penalty. All requests for substitutions must be submitted in writing to hcse2014 (at) s-lab (dot) uni-paderborn (dot) de.

Insurance

The registration fee does not include insurance of any kind. Participants and accompanying persons participate in all conference events on their own risk. The organizers cannot accept any responsibility or liability for injuries or losses sustained as a result of any cancellations, accidents, illness or other occurrences that may arise in connection with the conference or its evening events. Participants are advised to organize appropriate travel and health insurance themselves.

Place of Fulfillment

The court of jurisdiction is Paderborn. The right of the Federal Republic of Germany applies.
